Weekly DASH Diet Menu

Follow the DASH Diet for healthy blood pressure management and improved overall health!

Day 1: Monday

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with fresh berries and almonds.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cucumbers, and olive oil dressing.
  • Snack: Low-fat yogurt with honey.
  • Dinner: Salmon with steamed broccoli and quinoa.

Day 2: Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and a poached egg.
  • Lunch: Turkey and vegetable wrap with whole wheat tortilla.
  • Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus.
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables and brown rice.

Day 3: Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond milk, and protein powder.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken with quinoa salad and roasted vegetables.
  • Snack: Apple slices with almond butter.
  • Dinner: Baked tilapia with sautéed kale and sweet potatoes.

Day 4: Thursday

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with chia seeds and fresh fruit.
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with a side of mixed greens.
  • Snack: A small handful of unsalted almonds.
  • Dinner: Grilled turkey burger with a side of roasted Brussels sprouts.

Day 5: Friday

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole grain toast.
  • Lunch: Shrimp and avocado salad with a citrus vinaigrette.
  • Snack: Sliced cucumber and bell peppers with guacamole.
  • Dinner: Grilled chicken with roasted zucchini and wild rice.

Day 6: Saturday

  • Breakfast: Whole grain pancakes with fresh strawberries.
  • Lunch: Quinoa and vegetable-stuffed bell peppers.
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with a side of asparagus and brown rice.

Day 7: Sunday

  • Breakfast: Chia pudding with berries and a drizzle of honey.
  • Lunch: Chicken and vegetable stir fry with whole wheat noodles.
  • Snack: A handful of mixed nuts.
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp with sautéed spinach and quinoa.
